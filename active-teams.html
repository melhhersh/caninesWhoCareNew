Meet Our Active Teams!

Jaime & Tito

Jaime’s passion for therapy dog work began during her time as a nurse at Brackenridge Hospital, where she witnessed the powerful impact of Jack, the trauma dog, in bringing comfort and calm during difficult moments. Inspired by that experience, she set out to bring the same support to schools and the community. Tito joined the Margiotta family at just five weeks old and quickly showed his calm, intuitive, and people-focused nature. Today, he provides comfort and connection to students, staff, and community members alike. Together, Jaime and Tito embody the mission of Canines Who Care bringing comfort, building trust, and supporting others when it matters most.

Mandy & Ruthier

Ruthie is a Staffordshire Terrier Mix, who was adopted from Footbridge Foundation in San Antonio, Texas. Ruthie is pure joy and love! She loves peanut butter and snuggling on the sofa. She loves going to the schools and letting the kids love on her. Mandy works hard as an aesthetician to give the five rescue animals in her house the best life they can have!

Jyl with Leo & Dash

Jyl has done lots of stuff - radio broadcaster, Celtic musician, English teacher, and working in the Texas heat training hundreds of dogs for other people, Finally she and her collie Vito joined Canines Who Care in 2016 and she ended up being the trainer (and cheerleader) for the group. Her current PERFECT dogs are Dash - whose blue eyes will steal your soul, and Leo - who fixes broken hearts. She dedicates this work to her former partner, Vito, who adored affection and loved being part of a team that brings love and joy to others. Credentials: CPDT-KA, Level 3 Tellington TTouch Companion Animal Practitioner, AKC CGC Evaluator

Deidre & Rowdy

Diedre is a longtime resident of Hays County who finds true joy in giving back to her community. Alongside her is Rowdy Ray! Her always happy GSP with a big personality and an even bigger heart. When Rowdy isn't bringing smiles and comfort to those around him, you can find him splashing in the water or on the hunt for birds, squirrels and lizards. Together, they make a compassionate team dedicated to spreading kindness wherever they go.

Lucienne & Nelson

Nelson is an 8 yr old Miniature Poodle and has been sharing his sweet sassy brand of love in therapy situations since he was barely a year old. Saying that Nelson is “food motivated” is an understatement, and he will do pretty much anything for a treat! He loves to show off his tricks and to make you smile. I love seeing how Nelson's sweet and gentle personality can brighten someone's face and maybe make their day a little easier. Dogs really can accomplish what humans can't. We are thrilled to be joining the Canines Who Care team!

Stephanie with Kelly & Nelly

Lauren & Coco

She’s a 9 year old German Short Hair pointer mix with the most loving, gentle spirit. Coco has an incredible ability to sense people’s emotions, she always seems to know when someone needs comfort and is right there by their side. John & Sophie

My name is Sophie and my favorite thing in the world is visiting with my friends, whether it be the daily walkers at the fence, a nurse at the hospital having a rough day, or a stressed student about to go into a test. These are my friends and I need to share some love to melt away their life’s daily stresses. And, I have a few tricks up my sleeve to get it done - literally. I love to dress up for the occasion, any occasion. Silly antics always bring a smile. Be my first contact of the day and I’ll show you my patented belly crawl greeting. Need a fist bump to celebrate an accomplishment. I’m there for you. Want to take me home with you? Ok, I have to draw the line somewhere. But, ask me for a trading card and sticker and you’ll have a keepsake to remember our visit the next time you could use a little puppy love.
